Who We Are

Learning Web Club (LWC) is a passionate community based at the Jordan University of Science and Technology. We are dedicated to teaching, building, and innovating in the world of web development. Our mission is to prepare the next generation of developers through hands-on workshops, inspiring events, and practical projects that shape the digital future.

With a strong belief in collaboration and creativity, our team brings together students from all backgrounds who share a love for coding, designing, and solving real-world problems through technology.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, LWC offers a space to grow your skills, share knowledge, and make lasting connections.
